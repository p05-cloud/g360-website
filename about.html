The Visionary

Prakash More

☸ "Believe in Karma"

Prakash More is the driving force behind G360 - Guru 360, representing a 360-degree approach to fitness and wellness. With over two decades of unwavering dedication to the fitness industry, Prakash has transformed countless lives and established himself as one of India's most respected fitness professionals.

His philosophy, "Believe in Karma," reflects his core belief that consistent effort, dedication, and positive action lead to transformative results. This principle guides everything at G360 - from training methodologies to brand partnerships.
